How It Works

The process for making your dream mobile app is planned and executed in the most efficient way possible.

Step 1. Plan ($25)

The beginning of the process where I identify requirements of the app. Also known as the “consultation”. This usually in the form of a business call or in person meeting, depending on the clients location. This part of the process is used to create the scope of the project. Scope is the defined features and functions of the product. An estimate of how long the project will take is also determined here.

Step 2. Design ($200)

This is where I contact one of my designers to produce visuals of each page in your app. This includes buttons, text, icons, colors, fonts, etc. If the visuals are not to your liking, I will reach back out to my designer until you are satisfied with how your app looks.

Step 3. Develop

With the scope established as well as the designs finalized, I can now begin to code your project. I code in Flutter, which allows me to create iOS and Android apps simultaneously, so you never have to worry about either platform having differences.

Step 4. Test

To ensure your app is working correctly, we will test your app thoroughly during this period. This includes testing among a select group of users that you specify, usually by email.

Step 5. Deployment

Once the application has completed all previous steps with satisfactions, it is now time to upload the app to the market to be public for everyone. Deployment for Android apps usually takes 1-3 days, whereas an iOS app may take 3-7 days. Things like app description, app icon, privacy policy, support url, and anything else to describe the app is needed here.
